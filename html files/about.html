about us

Slogan: GETHSEMANE! Response The word of God z our power and vise versa... Motto: seek first the kingdom of God and it's righteousness and every other thing will be added into u mean while we use it as "seek first the kingdom of God...."Matthew 6:33

Gethsemane ministry was an inspiration of the holy spirit to a great lover of the things of the spirit.The name "GETHSEMANE" was inituated of the holy spirit. Gethsemane was the garden where the decison was concluded that christ would die for all. It is the garden of sober reflection and ofcourse, the only place christ bled blood. The ministry took it name and full course around June 2017 in Abakaliki, Ebonyi State by the direction of the holy spirit. The Ministry has been in operation for the last Five years under the leadership of Evag David Nwiboko and has been since the operating in Amike-aba of Abakalikiof Ebonti state. God has been Faithful with the Ministry having heir own website, Social-media pages all can be found on our Website.The ministry has been operating on no offering pattern for the past five years of existence.
